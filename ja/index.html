Which exchanges does TradeArk support?
TradeArk supports Binance, OKX, Bybit, Bitget, and Gate.io -- covering spot, perpetual futures, and swap trading across all major cryptocurrency pairs. More exchanges are being added regularly.
Can I use AI agents with TradeArk?
Yes. TradeArk provides a full HTTP API that AI agents like OpenClaw, Claude Code, and Codex can use for automated trading. The built-in risk firewall lets you set per-agent limits, pre-trade rules, and kill switches so your AI trades within safe boundaries.
Which port does TradeArk use?
By default, TradeArk uses port 38182. Open http://127.0.0.1:38182/ on your local machine. To access from another device on your network, open TCP port 38182 in your firewall first.
How do I stay safe with AI automated trading?
Start with demo or testnet mode. Configure the risk firewall with position size limits, per-agent token budgets, and pre-trade policy rules. Enable manual confirmation for real orders until you are confident in your AI strategy.
Is TradeArk free to use?
Yes, TradeArk is free to install and use locally. You only pay exchange trading fees which go directly to the exchange -- TradeArk does not charge any trading fees or commissions.
